DyaPlex is NVIDIA's full-duplex speech-motion model for dyadic interaction. It brings streaming speech and physical motion into one interactive system, allowing an agent to listen, speak, perceive a partner's motion, and generate synchronized body-motion responses in real time.

The project highlights the next step from speech-only full-duplex agents toward multimodal embodied interaction, where conversational behavior depends on both audio and partner motion.
