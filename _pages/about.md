I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering,  [HKUST](https://hkust.edu.hk/) advised by [Prof. Qifeng Chen](https://cqf.io/).

Before joining HUKST. I spend two wonderful years in Huya as a computer vision engineer led by [Xintong Han](https://xthan.github.io/) and [Jia Xu](https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~jiaxu/). 

I was an intern at Tencent AI Lab advised by [Yibing Song](https://ybsong00.github.io/) and [Wei Liu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=AjxoEpIAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). 

Meanwhile, I have cooperated many times with my good friend [Ziyu Wan](http://raywzy.com/).

My research interest includes *2D/3D Generation, digital human, neural rendering and contrastive learning*. 

**I love fishing very much, and my dream is to go fishing in all over China!**
